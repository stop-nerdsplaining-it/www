What is „nerdsplaining“?

You probably have heard of mansplaining: „[...] a pejorative term meaning (of a man) to comment on or explain something to a woman in a condescending, overconfident, and often inaccurate or oversimplified manner“ (Wikipedia).
„Nerdsplaining“ is the more diverse, the more inclusive term for this unpleasant behavior as it does not focus on gender.
And even if the domain suggests it: of course it is not exclusive to IT people.
